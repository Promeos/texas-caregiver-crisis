Dear Open Records Coordinator:

Pursuant to the Texas Public Information Act (Texas Government Code, Chapter 552), I am requesting the following records from the Health and Human Services Commission, Provider Finance Department:

### Records Requested

**1. ICF/IID Provider Cost Reports**

All Intermediate Care Facilities for Individuals with an Intellectual Disability or Related Conditions (ICF/IID) Medicaid cost reports submitted to HHSC Provider Finance Department via STAIRS (or STEPS, if applicable) for the following cost reporting periods:

- Fiscal Year 2024 (September 1, 2023 – August 31, 2024)
- Fiscal Year 2022 (September 1, 2021 – August 31, 2022)
- Fiscal Year 2020 (September 1, 2019 – August 31, 2020)

For each provider, I request all tabs/sections of the cost report as submitted, including but not limited to:

- Provider identification and facility information
- Revenue schedules (Medicaid and non-Medicaid)
- Expense schedules (direct care staffing costs, administrative costs, facility costs, contracted services)
- Staff hours and FTE counts by position category
- Census/utilization data (bed days, patient days, occupancy)
- Schedule D (building lease information)
- Schedule E (contract management information)
- Cost allocation worksheets
- Accountability report data (attendant compensation / direct care staffing enhancement)

**2. HCS and TxHmL Provider Cost Reports**

All Home and Community-based Services (HCS) and Texas Home Living (TxHmL) waiver program cost reports submitted to HHSC Provider Finance Department for the same three cost reporting periods listed above.

For each provider, I request all tabs/sections of the cost report, including but not limited to:

- Provider identification and program information
- Revenue schedules (Medicaid and non-Medicaid, by waiver program)
- Expense schedules (direct care staffing costs, administrative costs, transportation, contracted services)
- Staff hours and FTE counts by position category
- Service utilization data (units of service, individuals served)
- Schedule D (building lease information)
- Schedule E (contract management information)
- Cost allocation worksheets
- Accountability report data (Medicaid spending requirement under Riders 39 and 40)

### Format

I request that all records be provided in **electronic, machine-readable format** — preferably as exported data files (CSV, Excel/XLSX, or database extract) rather than scanned images or paper copies. If the data exists in the STAIRS or STEPS database, a data export from that system in its native tabular format would be ideal.

If electronic export is not feasible, I will accept PDF copies of the submitted cost reports.

### Scope and Clarifications

- This request covers **all non-state-operated** ICF/IID providers and **all** HCS/TxHmL providers that submitted cost reports for the specified periods.
- I am **not** requesting State Supported Living Center (SSLC) data.
- Provider cost reports are financial records of entities receiving public Medicaid funds. They do not contain protected health information (PHI) or personally identifiable client data. Provider names, addresses, contract numbers, and financial data are public information.
- If any portion of the requested records is considered exempt from disclosure, I request that the exempt material be redacted and the remainder be provided, as required by Section 552.302 of the Government Code.

### Fee Waiver / Cost Estimate

I request a waiver of fees for this request. The information will be used for public-interest research into the economics of Texas IDD services, including analysis of direct care worker compensation, provider financial sustainability, and Medicaid rate adequacy.

If a fee waiver is not granted, please provide a cost estimate before proceeding. I am willing to pay reasonable costs for electronic production of these records.

## Notes for Submission

1. **Biennial reporting cycle:** ICF/IID and HCS providers submit cost reports in even-numbered fiscal years. The most recent available reports should be FY 2024 (due April 30, 2025), FY 2022, and FY 2020.

2. **System transition:** HHSC is transitioning from STAIRS to STEPS beginning with cost reports collected in 2026. FY 2024 reports were likely the last collected via STAIRS.

3. **Expect pushback on volume:** There are approximately 850+ ICF/IID providers and 300+ HCS program providers. The data volume may trigger a cost estimate. Consider narrowing to a single year if costs are prohibitive.

4. **Alternative contact:** For questions about cost report data specifically, PFD can be reached at CostInformationPFD@hhs.texas.gov — but the formal TPIA request must go through the Open Records Coordinator.

5. **Timeline:** HHSC has 10 business days to respond. They may request an AG opinion to delay, which adds 45 business days. Budget 2-3 months for the full process.

6. **Partial alternatives already available:**
   - The Rider 4 Cost Comparison Report (Aug 2024) contains aggregate cost-per-individual data by setting and LON — already downloaded to `data/raw/official/cost-comparison-report-aug-2024.pdf`.
   - THRIP (Texas Healthcare Reimbursement Information Portal) at pfd.hhs.texas.gov was intended to provide public access to claims and cost report data for FY 2016-2021, but appears to be under reconstruction as of March 2026.
   - The 90% Medicaid spending requirement (Riders 39/40) means HHSC tracks whether each provider meets the threshold — this compliance data could be requested separately.
